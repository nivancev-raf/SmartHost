About SmartHost

Our Story

Founded in 2023, SmartHost emerged from a simple vision: to transform the way people experience short-term apartment rentals by combining cutting-edge technology with warm, personalized service.

What started as a small collection of premium apartments in Belgrade has grown into a trusted platform serving guests from around the world. We believe that every stay should feel like coming home, which is why we've carefully curated each property to meet our exacting standards of comfort, cleanliness, and convenience.

Our commitment to innovation drives us to continuously enhance the guest experience through smart home technology, seamless booking processes, and 24/7 support that's always just a click or call away.
